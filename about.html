Origin

From Backroom Mats To A Champion's Factory.

COMBAT 360 started in 2014 with eight mats, two heavy bags, and one obsession — building fighters the right way. A decade later, we're a 10,000 sq ft facility producing regional champions, professional athletes, and a community of people who simply refuse to be average.

Our DNA hasn't changed. The room is bigger. The coaches are deeper. But the standard is the same: show up, do the work, leave better than you came.
