Beginner Guide

Your Journey Starts Here.

Stepping onto the mat for the first time can feel daunting. At COMBAT 360, we've designed a clear path for absolute beginners to thrive, learn, and build confidence without pressure.

What to Expect:

A welcoming, ego-free environment focused on fundamental techniques. Our coaches prioritize safety, proper form, and a supportive atmosphere.

Your First Class:

Expect a dynamic warm-up, basic striking and grappling drills, and a cool-down. No sparring, just learning the ropes.

Gear Needed:

Just comfortable athletic wear and a water bottle. We provide gloves and hand wraps for your first session.

Ready to take the leap?

The hardest part is walking through the door. We make the rest easy.
